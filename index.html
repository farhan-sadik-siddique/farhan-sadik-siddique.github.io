Yes. Every order includes revisions until you're satisfied with the deliverable. I don't consider a job done until the output matches exactly what you asked for.

Reports as .docx or .pdf, SEO audits as .pdf, social media calendars as .xlsx or Google Sheets, email sequences as .docx. I can adjust based on your preference.
